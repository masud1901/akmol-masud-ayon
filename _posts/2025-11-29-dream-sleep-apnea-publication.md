Alhamdulillah, it's our immense pleasure to share that our latest work **"DREAM: A Novel Explainable Neural Network for Detecting Sleep Apnea Using Single-Lead ECG Signals"** has been published in the **Q1 Elsevier journal Biomedical Signal Processing and Control** (CiteScore: 11.5, Impact Factor: 4.9).

## Publication Details

- **Journal**: Biomedical Signal Processing and Control (Elsevier)
- **Impact Factor**: 4.9
- **CiteScore**: 11.5
- **Ms. Ref. No.**: BSPC-D-25-03525R1
- **Paper Type**: Research Paper

## Acknowledgments

Heartfelt congratulations to the team, especially **Sanjida Akter**, **Sanzida Islam Promi**, and **Akmol Masud Ayon** for their exceptional hard work. Sincere thanks to **Prof. Mohammad Abu Yousuf Sir** and **Mohammad Ali Moni Sir** for their guidance and support.

## Abstract Summary

The DREAM model presents a novel explainable neural network approach for detecting sleep apnea using single-lead ECG signals, making it more accessible and interpretable for clinical applications.

The accepted manuscript is currently being processed for publication and proofs will be available shortly.
