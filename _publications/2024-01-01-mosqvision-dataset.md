This dataset contains 3,000 high-quality annotated images of three major mosquito species (Culex, Anopheles, and Aedes) collected from multiple sources. The dataset is specifically designed for mosquito species classification tasks in computer vision and public health applications, with balanced representation across all species for robust model training.

[Access dataset here](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.14238701)

**Dataset Type:** Computer Vision, Entomology, Public Health, Species Classification
