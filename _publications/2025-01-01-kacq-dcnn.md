This paper presents KACQ-DCNN, a novel hybrid classical-quantum neural network architecture for heart disease detection. The framework combines Kolmogorov-Arnold networks (KAN) with classical-quantum dual-channel processing, incorporating uncertainty quantification and explainable AI techniques for robust medical diagnosis.
